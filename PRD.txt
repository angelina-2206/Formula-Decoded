Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Name:

Formula Decoded

Tagline:

Breaking Down the Technology of Modern Formula One

1. Product Overview

Formula Decoded is an interactive web platform designed to visually explain the engineering, aerodynamics, and hybrid power systems of modern Formula One cars, with special emphasis on the upcoming 2026 regulations introduced by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile.

The website will feature a high-fidelity interactive 3D Formula One car model, immersive animations, and scroll-driven storytelling to break down complex racing technologies into visually engaging experiences.

The project primarily focuses on advanced frontend UI/UX, animation systems, and interactive visualization rather than backend-heavy functionality.

2. Product Vision

To create a visually immersive digital experience that allows users to explore the engineering of a Formula One car as if they are dissecting a machine piece by piece.

The platform aims to transform complex motorsport engineering concepts into interactive visual narratives.

3. Objectives
Primary Objectives:

Demonstrate advanced frontend development skills
Showcase 3D web interaction using WebGL
Create a portfolio-level UI/UX experience
Educate users about Formula One technology visually

5. Core Features

The centerpiece of the website is a fully interactive 3D Formula One car model.

Users can explore the car and interact with its components in real time.

Capabilities

Zoom in on components

Scroll-driven camera transitions

Component information panels

Technology

Three.js

React Three Fiber

Modes

Corner Mode

Increased downforce

Higher wing angles

Straight Mode

Flatter wing positioning

User Interaction

Users can toggle between aerodynamic modes to see:

Project Summary

Formula Decoded aims to transform the engineering complexity of Formula One into a visually engaging interactive experience. By combining modern frontend technologies, immersive 3D graphics, and storytelling-driven UI design, the platform will provide users with a unique way to explore the machines behind the fastest motorsport in the world.
